What is AssetShare?

AssetShare is a groundbreaking online platform that connects owners of heavy-duty construction machinery with contractors and individuals in need of rentals. By simplifying and automating the rental process, AssetShare eliminates lengthy wait times, streamlines quotations, and enhances efficiency for both machine owners and renters. Whether it’s for construction or mining, our platform ensures machinery is utilized effectively while saving time and reducing administrative hassle.

Who we are

We are a team of industry professionals passionate about leveraging technology to transform the construction and mining rental sectors. At AssetShare, our mission is to bring efficiency, transparency, and value to equipment rentals. By combining our expertise with innovative solutions, we aim to create a centralized marketplace where machinery owners and renters can seamlessly connect and achieve mutual success.

What we do

AssetShare provides a dynamic platform that simplifies the entire machinery rental process. We offer machinery owners the opportunity to list their equipment and maximize utilization during idle periods, while renters can easily search, compare, and secure the machinery they need in real-time. From generating quotations and managing availability to handling payments and reporting, we take care of the details so our users can focus on getting the job done.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AssetShare?

Assetshare is an online marketplace that connects construction companies and contractors with machinery owners. Our platform enables users to rent a wide range of construction machinery, ensuring efficiency and cost-effectiveness in project execution.

How does the escrow feature work?

Our escrow feature provides a secure payment system. When a contractor rents machinery, the payment is held in escrow and is only released to the machinery owner once the equipment has safely arrived at the contractor's site. This ensures that both parties are protected against any risks.

What types of construction machinery can I rent on Assetshare?

You can rent a wide variety of machinery, including excavators, loaders, bulldozers, tipper trucks, and more. We offer equipment from multiple categories to meet the diverse needs of construction and mining projects.

How do I list my machinery on Assetshare?

Listing your machinery is simple. Sign up as an owner, complete your profile, and upload the details and images of your machinery. Once your listing is approved, it will be visible to potential renters.

What are the benefits of using Assetshare as a contractor?

Contractors benefit from competitive pricing, a wide selection of equipment, and our secure escrow system that ensures payment safety. Additionally, our platform provides a user-friendly experience for easy navigation and transaction management.

How does Assetshare ensure the quality of listed machinery?

Assetshare requires all machinery owners to provide detailed information and images of their equipment. We also encourage renters to review and rate their experience, ensuring transparency and accountability.

What is the process for renting machinery on Assetshare?

Browse the available machinery, select the equipment that suits your needs, add a rental period, and send a rental request. Once the owner approves, you make a payment that is securely held in escrow until the machinery arrives at your site.

What happens if the machinery does not arrive on time or is not as described?

In such cases, the escrow feature protects your payment. You can raise a dispute through our platform, and our support team will work with both parties to resolve the issue.

Are there any fees for using Assetshare?

Assetshare charges a nominal service fee on successful rentals to cover platform maintenance and support. The fee is clearly indicated during the transaction process.

How do I contact Assetshare support?

You can reach our support team via email at info@assetshare.co.za or through the live chat feature on our website. We are here to assist you with any questions or issues.
