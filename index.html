What is AssetShare?

AssetShare is a groundbreaking online platform that connects owners of heavy-duty construction machinery with contractors and individuals in need of rentals. By simplifying and automating the rental process, AssetShare eliminates lengthy wait times, streamlines quotations, and enhances efficiency for both machine owners and renters. Whether it’s for construction or mining, our platform ensures machinery is utilized effectively while saving time and reducing administrative hassle.

Who we are

We are a team of industry professionals passionate about leveraging technology to transform the construction and mining rental sectors. At AssetShare, our mission is to bring efficiency, transparency, and value to equipment rentals. By combining our expertise with innovative solutions, we aim to create a centralized marketplace where machinery owners and renters can seamlessly connect and achieve mutual success.

What we do

AssetShare provides a dynamic platform that simplifies the entire machinery rental process. We offer machinery owners the opportunity to list their equipment and maximize utilization during idle periods, while renters can easily search, compare, and secure the machinery they need in real-time. From generating quotations and managing availability to handling payments and reporting, we take care of the details so our users can focus on getting the job done.
